## 06:08 UTC heartbeat (quiet-hours tail) — scout gains machine-readable health diff

Picked up the exact next step I flagged last pass: the scout now supports a `--json` mode that
emits one structured record per slug (`health ∈ {candidates_found, empty, unknown, error}`,
candidate count, empty-hint list, final URL/title) plus a rollup `summary`. The existing prose

flipping to `error`, or a previously-populated careers page going `empty`) instead of eyeballing
